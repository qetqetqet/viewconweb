Hong Kong Metropolitan University Expands its Investment in EditShare

Nursing and Health Study of Hong Kong Metropolitan University (HKMU) integrated with EditShare Automated MAM System.

A highly-customized and automated MAM system with nearline storage and tape-based backup system for Nursing and Health Study of Hong Kong Metropolitan University (HKMU) has been installed and integrated with their nursing SimCap system. We have used AppleScript in the customization and automation in this project. According to EditShare, this is the very first case of EditShare Systems integrated with SimCap system successfully in the world! More information can be found at URL :
