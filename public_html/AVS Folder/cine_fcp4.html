2. New Real-Time Effects and Support
Real-time effects from uncompressed 8, 10 and 16-bit sources
Real-time keyframeable time remap
Real-time effects support with multiple formats in the same timeline while maintaining video out
Keyframeable real-time embedded moving alpha
Support for real-time effects in 24 (or 23.976) FPS
Support for real-time effects with live video out for both DV and Photo-JPEG in CinéWave sequences.
Complimentary support for Final Cut Pro 4's new CPU driven OpenRT multi-stream real-time effects

New high-precision 32-bit floating-point rendering with Final Cut Pro 4's HDR Image Processing Engine
Support for "Trillions of Colors" RGBA rendering with Adobe After Effects®

Support for multiple formats (Photo-JPEG, DV, CinéOffline and full uncompressed 8, 10 or 16-bit clips all within the same timeline without rendering)
