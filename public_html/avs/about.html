Viewcon was founded in 1995 by a number of network consultants and was appointed as an Apple Authorized Retailer in the same year. Focusing on providing solutions to Mac market with outstanding achievement, Viewcon was further appointed as an Apple Authorized System Retailer in 1998. With expertise in multimedia production, supporting content creation professional in audio and video production, Viewcon has been appointed as Valued Added Reseller in Music and DV in 1999 and 2001 respectively. Also, Viewcon has established its own production house, FireChannels Production Co. in 2000, which can be served as a demo room, besides commencing production business.

Due to the substantial configuration changes of PowerMac G3 compared to the previous generation of PowerMac, Viewcon has tried the best to source various products in order to bridge up the gap between new machines and existing peripherals. Viewcon has introduced and carried the distributorship for various products, e.g. ATTO, Automatic Duck, Griffin Technology, OrangeMicro, Rorke Data, GeeThree, Midiman, M-audio, Farallon and Proxim.

Recently, Viewcon has spent much effort in the merging market of digital video. Viewcon has carried distributorship for professional video editing systems, including Matrox RTMac, Digitalvoodoo, BlackMagic Design's DeckLink series and Pinnacle Systems CinéWave, meeting requirements of various range of professional video users.

In the course of integration and implementation of various professional video and audio systems, Viewcon has identified that there is a great demand for user training. Therefore, Viewcon has been organizing regularly a series of free seminars as well as training courses in pay basis. They are all well received by general public and professional users in the field.

As seen above, besides some general users of i-Products from Apple, Viewcon’s major customers are Mac users from professional markets ranging from graphic design, audio and video production, to 3D CG animation production, even including Hong Kong Government, universities and educational institutions, commercial corporate, professional studios and famous artists.

In 2003, Viewcon has further established a wholly-owned subsidiary, AVS Digital Professional specialized for video systems and network market. For simplicity, Viewcon will continue to focus in the professional video content creation and delivery market by providing the best and the most cost effective systems of software, hardware, training and support with its expertise and professionalism.
