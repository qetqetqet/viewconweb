DeckLink

Blackmagic DeckLink has been specifically designed to make connecting a Macintosh™ workstation to any SDI based broadcast digital deck, such as Sony Digital Betacam™, D1™, Panasonic DVC Pro 25™, DVC Pro 50™, D5, Sony™ J series. All digital decks with industry standard SDI connections are supported, and setup is quick and easy.

By using SDI connections, Blackmagic DeckLink contains video, and all audio channels down the single BNC type video cable. A Sony™ RS422 compatible deck control port is also provided making setup quick and easy. In addition to the SDI input, SDI output and RS422 connections for the deck, Blackmagic DeckLink also includes SDI and SPDIF audio outputs for independent monitoring.

When you don't need analog connections and are working in a fully SDI based environment, DeckLink is the perfect solution to deliver all the features you need at the lowest cost.

All cards in the DeckLink family have the most amazing real time effects, capture formats, and other great features. These are explained below.
DeckLink SP

Professional tape decks such as Sony Betacam SP™ are a broadcast industry standard, and now with DeckLink SP, you can work with high quality analog video direct with some of the most powerful editing and design software on the market today. After finishing your work, Betacam SP™ masters can be sent direct to broadcast stations or dub shops for duplication.

DeckLink SP includes some of the most advanced analog design techniques available today. The DeckLink SP design team includes some of the world's top analog designers and this means the advanced technology in DeckLink SP gives you much more for a dramatically lower cost.

Other DeckLink SP design innovations include an amazing 6 independent power supplies in a distributed power management system. This means each analog section of DeckLink SP can transport delicate analog signaling without interference from the digital stages in the video signal path.
Any professional video monitor can be directly connected to Decklink SP's SDI video output, or even the deck itself. The full 10 bit SDI video output can also be used to direct connect to high end decks such as Digital Betacam™ for mastering if required.

DeckLink SP also eliminates break out box clutter with our exclusive long 7 ft or 2 meter direct-connect break out cable that allows you to plug directly into your deck and monitoring. You don't even need to purchase cables, and if you want to disconnect your system, you only need to unplug a single cable.
DeckLink Pro

So even in situations where a deck is not available, DeckLink Pro allows you to monitor real time effects such as color correction at a very low cost. When a deck is available, simply plug it in, and capture all your video clips, and master out finished work in the most incredible uncompressed 10 bit video.

For audio purists, DeckLink Pro includes an incredible 8 channels of SPDIF digital audio output for 5.1 or 7.1 surround sound monitoring. This is a first for the industry, and no other uncompressed video card features this level of audio support. 8 channels of audio is perfect if you want to dub out audio to multi channel audio recorders such as the audio industry standard DA88 tape deck. 8 channel audio is also available via SDI embedded audio.

Featuring industry standard SDI and analog video monitoring, combined with the amazing 8 channels of audio, DeckLink Pro is a great choice when building a home based post production facility where you hire in decks when required. DeckLink Pro is just perfect when connecting to high end SDI based systems in the largest post facilities.

DeckLink Pro is low cost, so can even be used for simple video monitoring, when the deck is not available, or your working with non SDI based FireWire DV decks.

Connecting to a broadcast deck is simple and Blackmagic DeckLink Pro has been specifically designed to make it simple via dedicated SDI video and serial control connections. Compatible decks include Sony Digital Betacam™, D1™, Panasonic DVC Pro 25™, DVC Pro 50™, D5, Sony™ J series.

By using industry standard SDI connections, Blackmagic DeckLink Pro contains video, and all audio channels down the single BNC type video cable. DeckLink Pro also eliminates break out box clutter with our exclusive long 7 ft or 2 meter direct-connect break out cable to plug directly into your deck and monitoring. You don't even need to purchase any cables, and if you want to disconnect your system, you only need to unplug a single cable.
